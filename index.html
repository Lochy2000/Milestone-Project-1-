Benefits of Table Tennis


Social

Table Tennis is a great way to meet new people. The community is very welcoming and always happy to have new players.

Stay active!

People don't always asscoiate table tennis with exercise. But that is only because its so fun they forget its also exercise.

Brain exercise

Table Tennis is also a form of congnitive exercise. Practising hand eye coordintion, which has been linked to keeping the braing active.

Wellbeing

Here you can take your mind off any stress and just focus on the ball. Get lost in the rythem of the bounce.
