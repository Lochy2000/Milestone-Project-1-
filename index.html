Workshops

Here at The Art of Table Tennis we add a creative aspect to our club. By hosting weekly workshops where you can design and make your own table tennis bat.
First we create the bat, after you can design your print and add it to your bat.
Intrested? Check out our previous designs.

Training

Training is open to everyone whether you have completed the workshops or not. We Train twice a week from 18:30 - 20:30 on Monday's and Sunday's. All levels of ability are welcomed and accomedated for.
